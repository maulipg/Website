Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is from 12:00 PM onwards, and check-out time is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on availability and prior request.

Are meals included in the room rates?

Yes, basic meal plans (breakfast and dinner) are included in our room rates. Additional meal options or special dietary requests can be accommodated with prior notice.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days prior to check-in are fully refundable. Within 7 days, a 50% fee applies. No refunds for no-shows. Please contact us for specific cases.

Is there a security deposit required?

Yes, a refundable security deposit of ₹5,000 is required at check-in, which will be refunded upon checkout after deducting any damages or unpaid charges.

Are guests allowed to visit?

Yes, guests are allowed in common areas between 9:00 AM and 9:00 PM with prior registration at the reception. Overnight stays are not permitted.

What amenities are provided in the rooms?

Rooms come with beds, study desks, storage, fans, and Wi-Fi. Premium rooms include additional amenities like Smart TVs. Shared facilities include bathrooms and kitchens.
